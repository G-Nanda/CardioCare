What We Provide

  • Open Heart surgery

    open-heart surgery is to address serious heart conditions.

  • Aortic valve replacement

    It is a type of open heart surgery used to treat problems with the heart's aortic valve

  • Angioplasty

    It is a procedure used to open blocked coronary arteries caused by coronary artery disease.

  • Coronary artery bypass

    Medical procedure to improve blood flow to the heart. .
